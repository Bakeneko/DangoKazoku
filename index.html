Dango Kazoku

Use the mouse to drag or divide (doubleclick) dango.
Use up and down keys to add/remove dango.
Use left and right keys to change dango's skin.
Use + and - keys (num pad) to change dango's size.
You can shake the window to disturb the dango.
Some skins allow to merge dango.
